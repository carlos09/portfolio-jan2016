Who I am

Hello! My name is Carlos Esquer and I am a Web Designer & Developer. Born and raised in Southern California, I am based out of San Diego, CA. After graduating from Calexico High School in 2005, I decided to pursue a career in Web Design & Interactive Media at the Art Institute of California-San Diego. My learning did not stop there as I had the opportunity to really broaden my skillset by working at a highly ranked technology company. I had various roles and worked alongside some great peers that allowed me to grow personally and professionaly. I also spend time working on various side projects during my off time. If you want to know more about me, do not hesitate to get in touch!

Fredrix - Print Canvas

This is one of the sites that I contributed to while working with Fredrix. I helped develop this site along with their Director of Marketing. This was a new site that they needed launched.

This site neeeded a custom contact form email responder that depended on the users selection while they filled out the contact form. The contact form prompts the user to select as many brouchures as they wish to receive. Once the contact form is submitted, I set up a php script that checks the brouchure request and matches it each brochure pdf, and emails the user back instantly with it.

Another request that was needed for this site was a customer hub. This is something that is used in one of Fredrix's other sites that needed to be implemented into this one. They needed a custom membership system that allowed users to log in and see exclusive products in which they are able to download certain files.

Blush Desserts

Blush Desserts is a local bakery that has been making a big impact in San Diego. As an up and coming business, they needed a website where customers can go to to take a look at their menu, store location, and placing a catering request. I built out their website by using wordpress that allowed them the ability to easily update it as they wished. I also implemented some seo so that they can track and monitor the traffic coming to their site.

A lot of customers requested Blush to have a website so that they can compete with their competitors. A lot of people wanted to see what Blush had to offer and wanted to be able to see what kind of cakes and desserts they were able to create. I added the an online gallery for them, along with a dynamic instagram feed that would show all their new pictures they added.
